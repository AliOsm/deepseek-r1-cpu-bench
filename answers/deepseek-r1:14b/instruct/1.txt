**Step-by-Step Guide: Baking a Chocolate Cake from Scratch**

1. **Gather Ingredients:**
   - 2 cups all-purpose flour
   - 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
   - 2 cups granulated sugar
   - 2 large eggs (room temperature)
   - 1 cup vegetable oil (room temperature)
   - 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
   - 1 cup warm water
   - Baking soda and salt

2. **Preheat Oven:**
   - Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

3. **Prepare Pans:**
   - Grease two 8-inch round cake pans and line them with parchment paper.

4. **Sift Dry Ingredients:**
   - In a bowl, sift together 2 cups flour, 3/4 cup cocoa powder, 2 cups sugar, 1 teaspoon baking soda, and 1/2 teaspoon salt. Alternatively, whisk thoroughly if no sifter is available.

5. **Mix Wet Ingredients:**
   - In another bowl, beat 2 eggs with an electric mixer on medium speed until light and fluffy.
   - Gradually add 1 cup vegetable oil while continuing to mix.
   - Stir in 2 teaspoons vanilla extract.

6. **Combine Dry and Wet Mixtures:**
   - Add dry ingredients alternately to the wet mixture, starting with a portion of the dry mix, then some wet, repeating until all are combined. This prevents overmixing.

7. **Add Warm Water:**
   - Pour in 1 cup warm water and mix until well incorporated.

8. **Bake:**
   - Divide the batter evenly into the prepared pans.
   - Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

9. **Cool Cakes:**
   - Let the cakes cool in their pans for about 10 minutes, then transfer to wire racks to cool completely before frosting.

10. **Frosting Option (Optional):**
    - Prepare a simple chocolate ganache or buttercream. For example:
      - Ganache: Heat heavy cream and pour over chopped chocolate until smooth.
      - Buttercream: Mix powdered sugar, melted chocolate, and softened butter until light and spreadable.

11. **Assemble and Serve:**
    - Once cooled, frost the cakes as desired. Serve and enjoy!

This method ensures a moist and flavorful chocolate cake with accurate measurements and proper baking techniques.
