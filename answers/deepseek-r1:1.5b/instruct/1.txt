To bake a delicious chocolate cake from scratch, follow these organized steps:

### Ingredients:
- **Dry Ingredients**: All-purpose flour (2 cups), baking powder (1/4 teaspoon), baking soda (1/4 teaspoon), salt (1 teaspoon).
- **Wet Ingredients**: Salt (to taste), melted butter (2 sticks or 1 cup), eggs (6 large).
- **Creaming Agents**: Cream cheese unsalted (2 sticks) with vanilla extract (optional).

### Instructions:

1. **Prep the Oven**:
   - Preheat your oven to 375°F (194°C). Let it preheat for at least 10 minutes before baking.

2. **Mix Ingredients**:
   - In a mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt until smooth.
   - Gradually add the sugar, melted butter, eggs, cream cheese, and vanilla extract (if using) to the mixture. Mix well until just combined.

3. **Roll Out Dough**:
   - On a floured surface, roll out the dough to about 2 inches thick. If your pan is wider than recommended, use a sheet pan or cut it in half.
   - Place the dough in a greased baking dish and bake for at least 15-20 minutes until the edges are golden.

4. **Bake Cake**:
   - Preheat your oven to 375°F before baking. Bake the cake for 30-40 minutes if baked from scratch, or 15-20 minutes if preheating is done earlier.
   - Allow it to cool on a wire rack for at least 60 minutes.

5. **Decorate**:
   - Spread chocolate chips on parchment paper and spread them evenly on a serving plate. Arrange in clusters for a uniform look, or use chocolate sauce with chocolate buttercream for a richer texture.

By following these steps, you can create a flavorful and delicious chocolate cake that’s both satisfying and comforting to eat.
