The acronym LSTM stands for **Long Short-Term Memory**. It is a type of Recurrent Neural Network (RNN) that processes sequential data by maintaining information over long periods due to its memory cells and gates, which control the flow of information. This makes it particularly effective for tasks involving time series analysis and sequence modeling.
