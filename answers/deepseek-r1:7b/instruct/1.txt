### Chocolate Cake Baking Guide

#### Ingredients:
- 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter or oil
- 3/4 cup granulated sugar
- 2 large eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 cups whole milk (or substitute with almond or soy milk for nut-free)
- Water to make batter

#### Tools:
- Mixing bowls
- Whisk or electric mixer
- Measuring cups and spoons
- 9-inch round cake pan
- Parchment paper
- Wire rack
- Optional: frosting ingredients (vanilla buttercream or cream cheese frosting)

---

### Instructions:

1. **Preheat the Oven:**
   - Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). This temperature ensures an even baking process.

2. **Cream the Dry and Wet Ingredients Separately:**
   - In a large mixing bowl, beat the butter or oil, granulated sugar, and vanilla extract until creamy.
   - In another bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, salt, and baking powder until well combined.

3. **Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients:**
   - Pour the wet ingredients (butter/sugar/eggs/vanilla) into the dry ingredients bowl.
   - Mix just until combined to avoid overmixing, which can lead to a tough texture.

4. **Prepare the Cake Pan:**
   - Grease and flour an 9-inch round cake pan. Line it with parchment paper for easier removal.

5. **Bake the Cake:**
   - Pour the batter into the prepared pan.
   - Bake in the preheated oven for approximately 30-35 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

6. **Cool the Cake:**
   - Allow the cake to cool in the pan for about 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.

7. **Prepare Frosting (Optional):**
   - If desired, prepare vanilla buttercream or cream cheese frosting and spread it over the cooled cake.

---

### Tips:
- Ensure all ingredients are at room temperature for smooth mixing.
- Adjust the baking time if your oven varies in temperature; bake until golden brown.
- For a healthier option, consider substituting whole milk with almond or soy milk as needed.

Enjoy your homemade chocolate cake!
